Red Sea Haven

Where Golden Desert Hills Meet Crystal Blue Waves

AlMasa Hotel El Sokhna offers the ultimate luxury beachfront getaway, situated along the stunning Red Sea coast of Ain Sokhna. Only an hour's drive from Cairo, our resort offers a serene microclimate, year-round sun, and pristine sandy shores.

With 78 elegantly appointed rooms and suites, our property combines refined comfort with exceptional hospitality. Relax by the outdoor pool, unwind at the spa and fitness center, or savor a buffet feast at our Massive Restaurant overlooking scenic coastal waters.

Resort Experiences

Relax, Recharge & Explore by the Red Sea

At AlMasa El Sokhna, every day brings a new way to unwind. Bask on our private sandy beach, take a refreshing dip in the outdoor pool, or let the little ones enjoy supervised activities at the dedicated kids' club.

For those seeking rejuvenation, our spa and fitness center offer a full range of treatments and modern equipment. Adventurous guests can explore water sports and curated excursions along the stunning Ain Sokhna coastline. Free WiFi, 24-hour front desk, and complimentary parking ensure a seamless stay.
