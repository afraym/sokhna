Award-Winning 5-Star Escape

Your Slice of Paradise Where Golden Sands Meet Crystal Waves

Boasting an impressive "Very Good" (8.4/10) guest rating on Booking.com, AlMasa Hotel El Sokhna is your ultimate 5-star beachfront sanctuary. Nestled on the breathtaking Red Sea coast and just an hour from Cairo, step into a world of year-round sunshine, endless ocean breezes, and a picture-perfect private shoreline.

Wake up to guaranteed, sweeping sea views from every single room. Spend your days drifting in our sparkling outdoor pool, wandering through lush gardens, or indulging in ultimate relaxation at our world-class spa. When evening falls, treat your taste buds to an unforgettable culinary journey at the iconic Massive Restaurant against a backdrop of shimmering coastal waters.

Unforgettable Moments

Escape the Ordinary. Dive into the Extraordinary.

Celebrated by guests for its unbeatable location and pristine cleanliness, AlMasa El Sokhna crafts the perfect itinerary for your getaway. Sink your toes into the warm sands of our private beach, soak up the sun by our glittering outdoor pool, and let your kids' imaginations run wild with fun-filled activities at our dedicated kids' club.

Need to recharge? Let our expert therapists melt your stress away at the luxury spa, or keep up your routine in our state-of-the-art fitness center. Feeling adventurous? Dive into thrilling water sports along the famous Sokhna coastline. With high-speed Free WiFi, round-the-clock service, and complimentary parking, we've taken care of every detail so you don't have to.
